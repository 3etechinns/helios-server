Welcome to the {{settings.SITE_TITLE}} Server.

This site uses the Helios Voting System to enable individual voters to verify that their vote was correctly captured and tallied. Helios is a type of open-audit voting system, also called an end-to-end voting system.

Because Helios is built to be used on the Web, it is appropriate only for low-coercion elections. Specifically, Helios is not appropriate at this time for governmental elections where the stakes are particularly high, and the risk of one voter attempting to influence another is very real.
